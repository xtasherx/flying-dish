About flyingDish

Always on the go. Just like you.

We know all too well life is constantly moving and there's not always time to squeeze in a home-cooked meal and we've all turned to fast-food restaurants or microwave dinners. Let's be honest, all that fast and frozen food we stick in the micro before consuming is taking a toll on our body and health. This is why, here at flyingDish, we've come together to create a website that will allow you to search for delicious, home-cooked meals you can make in 30 minutes or less. We hope more meals that find your table come fresh from your kitchen.

"You don't have to cook fancy or complicated masterpieces, just good food from fresh ingredients."

- Julia Child
