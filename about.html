About flyingDish

Always on the go. Just like you.

We know all too well life if constantly moving and there is not always time to squeeze in a home-cooked meal. With this being said we turn to fast-food restaurants or microwave dinners. But let's be honest, all that fast and frozen food we stick in the micro before consuming is taking a toll on our body and health. This is why, we here at flyingDish, have come together to create a website that will allow you to search for delicious meals that can be made in under 30 minutes. We hope more meals that find your table come fresh from your kitchen.
